🏗 Design Principles

Built for objectivity and scale

Three structural decisions make the model objective, consistent, and manipulation-resistant from day one.

📈

Exponential ARR Baseline

ARR tiers jump exponentially 

(2 → 10 → 30 → 75 → 150 pts), not linearly. 
This naturally separates scores and eliminates statistical clumping .

🌐

Domain-Based Voting

Only unique account votes count as external upvotes.  Same account vote counts are isolated as low-weight "echo votes," neutralizing votes inflation.

✖️

Multiplicative Multipliers

Business factors compound multiplicatively. 

A strategic, at-risk account near renewal stacks all four multipliers — surfacing true urgency automatically.

V1.0 ● Active Now Now → January 2027

V1.0 Pure Data-Driven Formula

Runs entirely on deterministic data from Salesforce and internal databases. Zero subjectivity. Fully automatable from day one.

ER Score Formula

ER Score = Creator Account Score + Σ Other Customers Account Scores × 0.25 + Same customer Votes × 0.1

External upvotes from different company domains each contribute 25% of that supporter's account score — rewarding market breadth. Internal votes from the same company contribute a flat 0.1 pts each, capping the ballot stuffing impact regardless of volume.

Account Score Formula

Account Score = ARR × Customer Health × × Is strategic? × × Is an SF Contact? × × Milestone soon? ×

All multipliers compound. A strategic, at-risk enterprise account near renewal stacks ×1.50 × ×1.35 × ×1.20 × ×1.15 = ×2.79 on top of its ARR base — reflecting genuine business urgency.

V2.0 🗓 January 2027

V2.0 AI-Augmented Formula

V2.0 layers two automated AI modules onto the V1.0 score — adding competitive intelligence and support cost analysis without any manual input.

 ▶

V2.0 Score Formula

V2.0 Score = V1.0 Base Score × Competitor Multiplier (AI) × Support Multiplier (AI)

AI multipliers compound on top of the full V1.0 score. An ER that already scores high on business value, faces active competitive pressure, and drives high support costs will escalate significantly — surfacing the most defensively urgent requests.

🤖

Competitor Threat AI

Text parser that matches ER descriptions against competitive loss matrices and churn notes in Salesforce to detect feature gaps that are actively costing deals.

🔴 High — Actively losing deals or churn to named competitors due to this gap × 1.35
🟡 Parity Gap — Industry table-stakes; competitors offer it × 1.15
⚪ None — Architecture-specific or no competitive signal × 1.00
🎫

Support Ticket Clusterer

Maps the ER workflow against Support tickets related to the ER to quantify the operational support cost of not building this feature.

🔴 High Impact — Workflow triggers >20 tickets / month × 1.30
🟡 Medium Impact — 1-20 tickets / month × 1.15
⚪ Low / None —  × 1.00
